    Who wants to make their own flower arrangements? See our large selection of workshops you can participate in below!

    DIY Vase Arrangements Workshop

    We love to teach, and our vase arrangement workshops are the perfect way to bring a special group of people together to learn a newfound skill. Our instructor Claudia will teach you the ins and outs of a perfect and aesthetic floral arrangement!

    Flower Crown Workshop

    Immerse yourself in a world of blooms during our Flower Crown workshop, offering a hands-on experience like no other. Get ready to unleash your inner artist as you customize your flower crown to reflect your unique style and personality - and maybe match your outfit too! Our experts will provide step-by-step assistance, ensuring that each flower of your flower crown is made just for you.

    Pressed Flower Art Workshop

    Pressing flowers is a unique way to preserve the beauty of nature while also creating and preserving new memories. Learn how to press flowers you gather on nature walks, bouquets, or from your own garden! Our instructor Timmy will teach you the ins and outs of flower pressing from selecting the right flower, to creating your own framed floral artwork.

    Bouquet-Making Basics

    From aspiring floral designers, experienced florists and avid gardeners, to those who just really love flowers. We have a class for you! Our instructor Pablo will walk you through how to create the perfect bouquet whether it be for you're friends, family, partner or yourself!

    Floral Centerpiece Workshop

    We offer exclusive private workshops for groups, where attendees can craft beautiful floral centerpieces while also enjoying a fun atmosphere with friends or colleagues. This workshops is perfect for a variety of private events, including corporate gatherings, company events, team-building exercises, ladies night or any social event.
